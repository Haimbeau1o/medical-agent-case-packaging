# Deep-Dive Q&A (20)

## Format
Each item includes a short answer (~20s) and a deep answer (~90s).

1. Why not use a single LLM for direct routing?
2. What is the exact function of each layer?
3. How do you define OOD and measure it?
4. Why is offline replay still valuable for interviews?
5. How do you prevent scope inflation of metrics?
6. Why prioritize safety over aggregate Top1?
7. What are the top confusion pairs and why?
8. Where does P95 latency come from?
9. How do you reduce fallback over-triggering?
10. What triggers human escalation?
11. How do you verify escalation reliability?
12. How do compliance constraints affect design?
13. What does E6 mean operationally?
14. How do you avoid overfitting to replay data?
15. Why are rerank features needed in Layer 4?
16. How do you design rollback conditions?
17. What was your personal highest-leverage decision?
18. How did AI tooling accelerate your workflow?
19. What would you improve with one more week?
20. Which claims in your resume are strongest and why?

## Answering Rule
- Always start with scope and boundary.
- Then explain design and trade-off.
- End with reproducible evidence reference.
